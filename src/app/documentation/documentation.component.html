1.About

Established in 1985, Hope Enterprises has considerable knowledge regarding the design and management of research projects. In our three decades of existence, we have accumulated extensive experience in both quantitative and qualitative methods. Our focus includes feasibility studies; market dynamics surveys; formative, outcome and impact assessments; as well as corporate image surveys and staff satisfaction surveys. HOPE Syndicated Services Division was founded in 2004 in order to serve the syndicated research market. In 2005, Hope Enterprises opened a North American office in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. In a drive to maintain the rigor of the research process and to directly serve the eastern Caribbean, HOPE Research Trinidad Limited opened its office in Diego Martin, Trinidad in 2008. The company also established itself in India in 2016. The services offered by HOPE are carried out throughout the Caribbean and Latin American regions, Central America, North America, the United Kingdom, and India. In order to better serve the Caribbean Region and North America, HOPE has opened the following subsidiaries: HOPE Enterprises USA, HOPE Research Trinidad LTD and HOPE SSD. By taking into account emerging trends, along with our business model, we provide value for money, high quality support, and solve targeted issues for our clients. Our intensive and thorough groundwork, along with our innovative research ensures that we provide accurate insights and growth strategies. This helps our clients gain and retain their competitive edge, and increase their return on investment.
